Origin

It is a ritual among Satere-Mawe, a native tribe of the rainforest in Brazil, as an initiation rite in order for boys to become men. The tribe carries out the initiation by weaving bullet ants into a glove made out of leaves. A Satere elder blows smoke into gloves filled with bullet ants. A dozen young tribe members, with indigenous designs painted on their bodies, dance in a circle, one of the tribe members eventually plunging his hands into the bullet ant infest gloves. The bites are deep and direct into the central nervous ystem. After a time, he lets off a hurtful moan of pain, he withdraws from the chanting Indians dancing in a circle, he doesn't express agony as that shows weakness. The bullet ant initiation also known as Waumat, has been alive among the Satere-Mawe for centuries, this rite of passage is very important to the young tribe members and are expected to be leaders to resist against deforestation, land grabbers, and aging Bolsonro who took over Brazil.

The Sting of the bullet ant

The bullet ant sting is one of the most painful stings of any insect, the bullet Ant sting contains neurotoxin. The stinger is located on the ant’s abdomen and is 1-3mm long. One sting from this pest can leave a person hurting for 8 hours, the pain is described as an intense and lasting burning sensation, and is often accompanied by sickness and vomiting.

Where does it take place

Fortaleza, Para, Brazil—The ceremony starts in the village of Fortaleza, on the Andira River. When boys reach the age of 13, they enter the jungle to find and harvest bullet ants that will be used for the ceremony.

Religion

The Satere Mawe tribe are truly Christian’s, they have chapels of mud and clay, and in the 1600-1700 the Jesuits created Aldementos, missions among the Indians. In the article by Pime, the indigenous people were rummaged through a visitors bag saying, “We wanted to know if your were a true missionary, We have seen that in your briefcase there is the rosary, there is the chalice of the Mass, just as our elders had told us”. This can tell us that they are truly Christian’s.
